QuickPlan

This website allows students to plan their day quickly and efficiently. Creating an account and inputting assignments or tests that you need to complete runs your schedule through our algorithm based on data from other students and presents you with a plan that we suggest you follow to obtain your optimal grade. If you feel that our propositions are not suiting your needs, we allow you to make modifications to them - like manually changing the amount of time alotted to study for tests every day.
